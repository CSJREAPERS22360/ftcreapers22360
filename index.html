The Reapers is a student-led robotics team participating in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). The team is based at Forest Hills High School and consists of 18 members, primarily juniors and seniors. Team members collaborate across multiple functional areas, including robot design and engineering, software development, and community outreach, with responsibilities shared among all participants.

Our mission is to advance the values of FIRST by expanding access to STEM education, particularly for underrepresented students. We use robotics as a platform to develop technical skills, collaboration, and innovation.
