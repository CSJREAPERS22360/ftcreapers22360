Family

Team Reaper #22360 is a fourth-year FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) team based at Forest Hills High School in Queens, New York. Founded in 2022 to expand STEM access for underrepresented students, the team of juniors and seniors competes annually in FTC, designing, building, and programming robots. In its fourth year, the team advanced to the NYC Championships for the first time, earning the 2nd Place Inspire Award and 1st Place Design Award. Beyond competition, Team Reaper mentors students at J.H.S. 157 Halsey, Russell Sage, and P.S. 220, leads robotics workshops at the Queens Public Library, and collaborates with Paramount Global to deliver large-scale STEM events reaching over 1,000 students.
